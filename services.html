Our Services

Comprehensive statistical solutions tailored for academic and institutional research.

Descriptive Analysis

We transform raw data into clear, understandable summaries. This includes calculating means, medians, standard deviations, and frequencies to give you a bird's-eye view of your dataset.

  • ● Data Cleaning
  • ● Frequency Tables
  • ● Graphical Representation

Inferential Statistics & Hypothesis Testing

Go beyond simple summaries. We use advanced statistical tests (T-tests, ANOVA, Chi-Square, Regression) to determine if patterns in your data are real or just chance.

  • ● P-Value Calculation
  • ● Correlation Analysis
  • ● Regression Models
  • ● Confidence Intervals

Report Writing & Visualization

  • ● APA Style Tables
  • ● High-Res Graphs
  • ● Methodology Writing
  • ● Interpretation Guides
