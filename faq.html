FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

How long does the analysis take?

Our standard turnaround time is 10 business days. For urgent requests, we offer an expedited 5-day service for an additional fee.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We sign a strict Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) with every client. Your data is stored on encrypted servers and deleted 30 days after project completion.

Do you write the discussion section?

We provide a comprehensive interpretation of the results which serves as a strong foundation for your discussion. However, you will need to contextualize these findings within your specific literature review.

What software do you use?

We primarily use SPSS, R, and Python depending on the complexity of the analysis required.
